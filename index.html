Who Were the Samurai?

Samurai were the noble warriors of feudal Japan, renowned for their discipline, honor, and exceptional skills in combat. They adhered to the bushido code, which emphasized loyalty, courage, and self-sacrifice.

Key Features of Samurai:

  • Mastery in combat and swordsmanship
  • Strict adherence to the bushido code
  • Loyalty to their daimyo (lords)
